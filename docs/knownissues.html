Known issues

General issues

  • Linux/Mac users with nVidia drivers may find AssaultCube crashes due to buggy drivers.
  • The bots are fairly dumb and need improvements.

First-run issues

These are issues that only ever happen on the 1st load of AssaultCube and dissapear on the 2nd load.
  • It takes a bit longer than normal to load due to shadows.dat files being generated.
  • If you bypass pressing "apply" after changing settings, the settings listed won't change until after you restart AssaultCube.
